$$ {(n+3)! \over (n+1)!} $$

#### 콎e코en칤

$$ {n \ge -1  \cap n \in Z} $$

$$ {(n+3)! \over (n+1)!} = {(n+1)! * (n+2) * (n+3) \over (n+1)!} = (n+2)(n+3) = n^2 + 5n + 6 $$

### Uprav + podm.:

$$ {1 \over n!} - {1 \over (n+1)!} $$

#### 콎e코en칤

$$ n \ge 0 \cap n \in Z $$

$$ {1 \over n!} - {1 \over (n+1)!} = {n+1-1 \over n!(n+1)} = {n \over (n+1)!} = {1 \over (n-1)(n+1)} = {1 \over n^2 - 1} $$
